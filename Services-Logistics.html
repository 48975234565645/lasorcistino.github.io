    Guidelines

  • Contracts are to be made to "Polar Shipping and Logistics"
  • The m3 restriction is 350.000 (350k m3) per contract.
  • Exports (0.0/low-sec to high-sec) must be from a blue character.
  • Imports (high-sec to 0.0/low-sec) can be from any alt.
  • The completion in time should be set to 7 days or more.
  • The expiration date should be set to 7 days or more.
  • 5 million ISK minimum reward per contract to/from primary destinations.
  • 25 million ISK minimum reward per contract to/from secondary destinations.
  • Contracts should never be made to systems with Cynosural System Jammers.
  • Contracts CANNOT feature assembled containers.
  • Jita cargo should be contracted to/from "Jita IV - Moon 4 - Caldari Navy Assembly Plant".
  • Amarr cargo should be contracted to/from "Amarr VIII (Oris) - Emperor Family Academy".
